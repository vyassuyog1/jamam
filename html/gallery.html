Maharashtrian Attire

My Love in a Maharashtrian Attire

She wore a sari in a Maharashtrian style and did a beautiful Lavani dance. If you could have seen my expressions, you would have said that might be his luckiest day. She was looking like the prettiest Maharashtrian girl ever.

She in Saree

She in Sari

She wore this sari for her freshers in college. This was the first time I had ever seen her in a sari outside of her home, and honestly, the sari became even prettier because of her beauty. When I saw her dance in that sari, I wished and prayed to God: please make her mine.

Favorite Dress

She in My Favorite Dress

This dress is one of my most favorites of hers. Whenever I see her in this dress, it feels like maximum beauty lies in maximum simplicity — and that is totally applicable for her. Even in the simplest of attires, without designer clothes, she looks prettier than all the girls present in the universe.

Her Smile

Her Smile

This is a beautiful close-up of her smile. I won’t be exaggerating when I say her smile is the greatest creation of God. Even if I write poems, make websites, paragraphs, or books about it, they would never do justice. If you have her smile in your phone, congratulations — you’ve achieved something rare. And for me, I’m proud of that.
